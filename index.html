About NAGNU

NAGNU is a free software project designed to promote simplicity, transparency, and freedom. Inspired by the GNU project, but remastered.

Philosophy

We believe in software that respects your freedom, does not spy on you, and is hackable by design. NAGNU is built by and for those who value control over their own computing.
